Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Introduction

Deblandeau Medical Aesthetic and Wellness, PLLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website deblandeau.com or contact us regarding our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information — name, email address, phone number, and similar identifiers you provide when filling out our contact or booking forms.
  • Health information — information you voluntarily share regarding your health history, goals, and treatment interests as part of consultation intake.
  • Usage data — information automatically collected when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and manage appointments and consultations
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested information
  • To improve our website and services
  • To communicate updates, appointment reminders, or promotions (you may opt out at any time)
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Health Information & HIPAA

Any protected health information (PHI) you share with us is handled in accordance with applicable federal and Massachusetts state privacy laws, including HIPAA where applicable. We do not sell, rent, or share your health information with third parties except as required to provide your care or as required by law.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our website or business (e.g., scheduling platforms), under strict confidentiality agreements. We may disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our patients and staff.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and analyze site traffic. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, though some features may not function properly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at deblandeaumed@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
